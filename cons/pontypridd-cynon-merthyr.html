About Pontypridd Cynon Merthyr

Pontypridd Cynon Merthyr includes the towns of Pontypridd, Aberdare, and Merthyr Tydfil, situated within the former South Wales coalfields. These communities have a strong industrial legacy, with ongoing efforts to regenerate local economies and infrastructure. The area has long been a heartland of Welsh Labour politics.

Key Stats

  • Pontypridd Cynon Merthyr is formed by uniting the Westminster constituencies of Pontypridd and Merthyr Tydfil and Aberdare.
